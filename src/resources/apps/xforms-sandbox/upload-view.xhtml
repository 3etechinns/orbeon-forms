The OPS XForms Sandbox powers XForms documents everywhere: it allows you to easily try your own XForms documents, as well as XForms documents located anywhere on the web.

Just point to an XHTML + XForms document, press the "Run XForms" button, and check how the OPS XForms engine renders it!

NOTE: Your document has to be in the XHTML namespace. Plain HTML won't work.
